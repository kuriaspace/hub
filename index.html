About KuriaSpace

Built for the Kenyan tech community

KuriaSpace Tech Hub was born from a simple observation: Kenya has extraordinary tech talent, but not enough dedicated spaces where that talent can meet, collaborate, and be matched with opportunity.

We are building that space where developers, designers, product builders, and tech employers can gather, connect, and grow together. Whether you're a fresh graduate looking for your first role, a startup founder looking for talent, or an experienced engineer who wants to give back through training — KuriaSpace is your home.
